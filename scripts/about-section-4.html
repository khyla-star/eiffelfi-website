Why we built Lofty.

Founder

Jerry Chu

Founder & CEO

“When I tried to buy my first rental property, the process took months — flying to different cities, vetting managers, securing financing, doing the math on every deal. Most people never get past step one.

That’s why we built Lofty: so anyone could buy fractional ownership in a real rental property, earn rent daily, and sell when they want. No mortgage, no realtor, no months of paperwork.

Today, more than 40,000 investors own real estate through Lofty — many with their first real estate investment ever.”
